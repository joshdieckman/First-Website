Education

I graduated from Doss High School in 1999 with honors in the Pre-Engineering program. After I graduated, I was not interested in going to college, so I did not make it to college until late in 2013. I rationlized my decision as I was waiting to figure out what I wanted to do. I realized that I wanted to work in IT and that is what I am pursuing now.

I acquired my Associates of Science in Computer Information Technology in 2016 from Indiana Wesleyan University. I was able to maintain a grade point average of 3.9. That is something that I am very proud of. I am currently working towards my Bachelors of Science in Business Information Systems. I am hoping to eventually become a junior developer with my bachelors focusing on the back-end development.

I am a proud graduate of the Code Louisville program. I learned a lot during the 12 week course and I think it complimented my education beautifully. They use Treehouse which is a great resource and is very beneficial for anyone that is trying to learn any kind of programming language from beginners to the most experienced programmers.
